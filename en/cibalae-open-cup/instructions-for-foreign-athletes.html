Instructions for Foreign Athletes Competing in Croatia

Welcome! Please carefully follow the steps below to ensure your eligibility to compete in Croatia:

  1. Approval from Your National Powerlifting Federation

    Obtain a written approval from your National Powerlifting Federation permitting you to compete in Croatia. A template for this approval is provided below.

  2. Club Membership Application

    Complete the application form to join our club.

  3. Registration and Statement Form

    Fill out the registration form and the statement required for competing in Croatia.

  4. Medical Examination

    Submit a valid medical examination certificate that is no older than 6 months.

  5. Proof of Citizenship

    Provide a copy of your citizenship document from your country.

Important Note

You can disregard any instructions regarding submitting photographs.

Once all documents are completed, please send them together in a single email to plkgalacticos@gmail.com

Summary of Documents to Email

Please ensure the following documents are attached to your email:

  1. Written approval from your National Powerlifting Federation.
  2. Completed Club Membership Application Form.
  3. Completed Registration and Statement Form.
  4. Valid Medical Examination Certificate (not older than 6 months).
  5. Copy of your Citizenship Document.
