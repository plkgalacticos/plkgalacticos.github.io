Seminar

During the course of the weekend there will also be a seminar held by a world-renowned powerlifter and coach Emil Krastev. Emil is a two-time silver medalist and a two-time bronze medalist at IPF Worlds and a two-time Sheffield participant which makes him one of the very few people qualified to host this seminar.

The topic of the seminar is: What makes a world class powerlifter?

It will mainly focus on the mental aspect of being an athlete, from motivation to mindset. The seminar will be held between groups, so everyone will be able to listen, learn and ask any questions.
